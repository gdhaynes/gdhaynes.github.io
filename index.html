Welcome

DGD Services is a family owned light construction, design, and consulting company that has been offering quality services to customers in the Milwaukee area and beyond since 2011. We offer a full line of home improvement and repair services including:

  • Interior and exterior painting
  • Window, siding, and door replacement and repair
  • General carpentry
  • Handyman services
  • Home Inspection
  • Pressure Washing

In addition we offer design and prototyping services including:

  • 3D industrial CAD modeling
  • Architectural drafting
  • Thermoplastic 3D printing
  • Resin 3D printing

If you are interested please see the contact page for contact information.

About Us

DGD Services started in the summer of 2011 with three brothers painting houses as a summer job. Since then we have continued to operate as a small light construction company, doing everything from structural work to handyman services.
